Welcome to [ Tehfacebook ]

Tehfacebook is an online directory that connects people through social networks at colleges.

We have opened up Tehfacebook for popular consumption at Harvard University.

You can use Tehfacebook to:

  • Search for people at your school
  • Find out who are in your classes
  • Look up for friends' friends
  • See a visualization of your social network

To get started, click register. If you have already registered, you can log in.
